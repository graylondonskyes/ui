# Brand Preservation

Preserve logo files/URLs, imagery, CSS variables, color tokens, typography, product names, company names, contact blocks, legal/footer blocks, and existing visual signatures.

Where compatible, prefer dark premium backgrounds, gold/purple/neon accents, subtle glow, floating logo/hero treatment, glass/depth cards, crisp contrast, and premium CTA hierarchy.

Do not invent replacement brand assets or replace premium visuals with plain SaaS boilerplate.
